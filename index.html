Presentation

Among the famous questions of "how much i spent on league" or "Is league of legends as good as arcane" You might have thought one day: "How do i do the biggest amount of Damage" or "What is the best build"
Select your champion, your items and your target. League of damage will do the maths for you in order to know what's the best build.
DISCLAIMER : having the best dps doesn't necessarly means that you're going to deal the biggest amount of damage, other parameters must be taken in account (zoning, healing, dps windows)
This tool is only here to give you the most optimal build against one target
